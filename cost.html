The Cost of EATING OUT

We have found that it is almost 5x more expensive on average to get meals delivered from a restaurant than it is to cook at home. Cooking at home you are able to save the most money on protein-based meals.
Research from Forbes & Wellio

THE COST OF MEAL KITS

Meal kits can be a quick easy solution for many families, but they still come at a cost. Simple vegetable and pasta meals are can be very cheap to make at home, but be very costly thru meal kits. These types are mixed in with the others, still costing upwards of $12 a plate. This is regardless of what types of food you are getting. Although it is about half the price of a restaurant, it is still substantially more than eating out.

THE COST OF MEAL PREPPING

A home cooked meal is about $7.50 to $9.75 a person depending on the ingredients. Where a restaurant is an average of $34.60 per person. That's a savings of between $53 to $346.72 a month! Start your meal planning using some tips and recipies.
